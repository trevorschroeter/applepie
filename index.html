Tyson's Apple Pie Recipe

Because I lose it every year.

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

The History

On October 14, 2003, Trevor made a batch of Apple Pie, and it was delicious. He shared the recipe via email.

Thankfully Tyson took a picture of the email, because Trevor lost the recipe.

Trevor has basically made a tradition of losing the recipe and has relied on Tyson to send it to him every winter.

To celebrate ten years of Tyson safely keeping the recipe, and providing it on-demand, this page has been set up so that everyone can have access to the recipe without having to bug Tyson.

The Ingredients

2 quarts fresh apple cider
1 quart apple juice
1.5 cups white sugar
4 cinnamon sticks
3-4 cloves (optional)
1 pint Everclear

The Instructions

Place all ingredients in a pot
Bring to a boil
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes
Let cool
Remove cinnamon sticks and cloves
Add Everclear

The Notes

Stir occasionally until sugar is dissolved, otherwise it might burn on bottom of the pan

If you can't find fresh apple cider, use 3 quarts of Simply Apple juice - that works well.

Cinnamon sticks are expensive if you buy them from the spice aisle at the grocery store - Get them in bulk from Costco, or a mexican grocery store for about 1/4 the price.
